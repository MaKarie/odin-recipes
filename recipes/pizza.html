Italian Pasta Recipe

Homemade Pizza Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt/li>
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Homemade Pizza Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil. Knead until smooth (about 5–8 minutes).
  2. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  4. Roll out dough on a floured surface into your desired pizza shape.
  5. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  6. Sprinkle cheese and add your favorite toppings
  7. Place pizza on a baking sheet or stone and bake for 10–15 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Pour in pasta water a little at a time; toss.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
