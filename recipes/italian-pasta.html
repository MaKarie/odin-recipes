Italian Pasta Recipe

Italian Pasta Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)
  • Pasta water (reserve 1/2 cup)

Italian Pasta Steps

  1. Boil salted water in a large pot
  2. Cook spaghetti until al dente
  3. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
  5. Add garlic and saut√© until golden.
  6. Stir in red pepper flakes for 30 seconds.
  7. Add drained pasta to the pan.
  8. Pour in pasta water a little at a time; toss.
  9. Season with salt.
  10. Add chopped parsley; mix well.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!
